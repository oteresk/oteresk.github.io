About David Koster

Hello! Thank you for visiting my portfolio website!

My name is David Koster and I am currently a student at San Jose State University studying Digial Media Art. Currently, my focus is to enter the video game industry, however I also love to experiement with different forms of creativity. In the past several years, I have accumulated experience in several applications in the Adobe Suite, such as Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Lightroom, as well as various other programs, like Cinema 4D and Autodesk Maya. I am also pursuing a minor in Computer Science, where I have learned a handful of C++, Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I enjoy creating works that convey beauty, intrigue, or abstraction, however you may find other works that are simply made out of joy. Going forward, I am looking to expand my portfolio with video game projects, photography, and other projects I may create in my free time.
