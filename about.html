About

David Koster is an American Game Producer from San Diego, California and received his BFA in Digital Media Art from San Jose State University in 2021. Upon finishing his BFA, Koster achieved a Certified Scrum Master certification before starting his career. He is a motivated individual with experience in manual software testing, Scrum methodologies, and cross-cultural leadership. Koster holds a passion for video games, maximizing efficiency in workflows, and people. He likes to take on challenges and provide a better path towards a better result. He is currently residing in San Diego and open to work.

Koster began his career at Ronday Technologies, a virtual world startup, where he served as a QA Tester before being promoted to Assistant Producer. This position included assisting the Producer in workflow management, creation of release notes, manual software testing, creation of test plans. In his previous role at Indie MEGABOOTH, Koster served as Assistant Producer where he introduced project management tools and operational organization to the team, as well as cooperated with various independent game development studios.

Outside of his career, Koster enjoys travelling, photography, building computers, tinkering with technology, collecting vinyl records of his favorite musicians, and of course, playing video games.
